# Mistral Brand Reference

Use these rules for Mistral AI branded decks, PDFs, thumbnails, social cards, one-pagers, diagrams, and web/HTML content.

## Official Source

Brand page: https://mistral.ai/brand

The brand page states that Mistral AI logos, icons, name, trademarks, and identifying assets belong to Mistral AI and should be used responsibly. Prefer bundled official assets in `assets/mistral-brand-assets/`.

## Palette

Mistral Rainbow:

- Red: `#E10500` from RGB 225/5/0
- Orange Dark: `#FA500F` from RGB 250/80/15
- Orange: `#FF8205` from RGB 255/130/5
- Orange Light: `#FFAF00` from RGB 255/175/0
- Yellow: `#FFD800` from RGB 255/216/0

Beige:

- Beige Light: `#FFFAEB` from RGB 255/250/235
- Beige Medium: `#FFF0C3` from RGB 255/240/195
- Beige Dark: `#E9E2CB` from RGB 233/226/203

Black:

- Black: `#000000`
- Black Tinted: `#1E1E1E`

## Typography

- Use Arial throughout.
- Headlines should be plain, strong, and direct.
- Use tight editorial hierarchy: large title, compact subtitle, concise body.
- Avoid decorative typefaces.

## Visual Language

The 2026 presentation template uses:

- 16:9 landscape slides, 720 x 405 points in the source PDF. Extend this system to other formats by preserving the palette, grid, square geometry, typography, and M/rainbow accents.
- Cream/beige backgrounds with faint grid systems.
- Black or black-tinted top bands and text.
- Horizontal rainbow bars in red, orange, orange-light, and yellow.
- Pixel-like M marks and modular square geometry.
- Branded blocks, arrows, diagrams, icons, monochrome illustrations, color illustrations, pattern backgrounds, photography covers, and logotype slides.

## Logo Guidance

Available bundled asset groups:

- `mistral-logo/`: full Mistral wordmark variants.
- `mistral-rainbow-logo/`: rainbow logo variants.
- `m/`: standalone M icon variants.
- `m-boxed/`: boxed M variants.

Use:

- Full color/black logo on light beige backgrounds.
- White logo on black or photography backgrounds.
- Orange or rainbow M as a small slide anchor.
- Do not stretch, recolor, blur, crop, or place logos on low-contrast backgrounds.

## Do Not

- Do not create a mostly blue, purple, or generic tech palette.
- Do not use rounded pill-heavy UI unless content truly requires it.
- Do not rely on gradients as the primary brand cue.
- Do not use non-Arial type unless the user explicitly requests a different output system.
- Do not make scrollable slides or static assets with accidental overflow.
